Frequently Asked Questions

How secure is this password generator?

It uses cryptographically strong randomness via crypto.getRandomValues(). All generation happens locally in your browser.

What makes a strong password?

Length + complexity. We recommend at least 16 characters with a mix of uppercase, lowercase, digits, and symbols.

Are my passwords stored or sent anywhere?

No. Everything runs locally in your browser. Nothing is saved, stored, or transmitted.

What does "avoid ambiguous" do?

It excludes characters like i, l, 1, L, o, O, 0 that look similar to reduce confusion when reading.
