Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our Word Counter tool

How accurate is the word count?
Our word counter uses advanced algorithms to accurately count words by splitting text on whitespace and filtering empty strings, ensuring precise results every time.
How is reading time calculated?
Reading time is calculated based on an average reading speed of 200 words per minute, which is the standard for adult readers.
Is my text data secure?
Yes! All text processing happens locally in your browser. Your text never leaves your device and is not stored on any servers.
Can I use this on mobile devices?
Absolutely! Our word counter is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.
Does it work offline?
Yes, once the page loads, you can use the word counter even without an internet connection since all processing is done locally.
What file formats can I download?
Currently, you can download your text as a .txt file. The download includes your original text with a timestamp in the filename.
