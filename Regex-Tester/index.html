Frequently Asked Questions

Does this tool support all regex features?

Yes, it uses JavaScript's native RegExp engine which supports most PCRE features including groups, lookaheads, and backreferences.

What regex flags are available?

g (global), i (case-insensitive), m (multiline), s (dotall), u (unicode), y (sticky).

Is my data sent anywhere?

No. Everything runs locally in your browser. No data is transmitted or stored.
